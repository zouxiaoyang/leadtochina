Place & Transport: Arrive Shanghai
Accommodation: Holiday Inn Express Shanghai Zhabei

Day 2 Wed 3-Apr-13

Place & Transport: Shanghai
Accommodation: Holiday Inn Express Shanghai Zhabei
Today's Activities: Yuyuan Garden,Shanghai Old Street,Oriental Pearl TV Tower,The Bund When tour is over, you will be transferred back to your hotel by tour guide and driver.
Meals: B,L

The Bund (2 hours):Situated in the eastern part of Huangpu District, Shanghhai, the Bund runs along the western bank of the Huangpu River, centres on a section of Zhongshan Road. At the west side of the Bund, there are 26 buildings in diverse architectural styles, such as Baroque,Gothic, Classicism, Romanesque and the Renaissance etc.

Oriental Pearl TV Tower (1 hour):Situated in Pudong Park in Lujiazui, Shanghai, Oriental Peaarl TV Tower is the world's third tallest TV tower with a hight of 468 meters only after towers in Toronto, Canada and Moscow, Russia.

Yuyuan Garden (1.5 hours):Situated in the Anren Street of Shanghai and covering an area of about 20,000 square meters, Yuyuan Garden was finished in 1,577 by a government officer named Pan Yunduan for his parents as a place for them to enjoy a quiet and happy time in their old age and now Yuyuan Garden has become a popular place for both the locals and tourists.

Shanghai Old Street (1 Hour):Shanghai Old Street, known as Miaoqian Ave in the past, is an old business street of tourist attractions, shops, entertainment and cultural exhibits. The earliest bank, gold shop, jewler's store, wine shop and tea house still can be seen here. In the style of Ming Dynasty, the architecture here is elegant with classic simplicity with red poles, pink walls and black roofs with up-swept eaves. Moreover, there are several European buildings.

Day 3 Thu 4-Apr-13

Place & Transport: Shanghai
Accommodation: Holiday Inn Express Shanghai Zhabei
Today's Activities: Jade Buddha Temple,Shanghai Museum,People Square,Xin Tian Di,Old French Concession
Meals: B,L

Jade Buddha Temple (1 hour):In Jade Buddha Temple, there are two imposing jade Buddha statues which had been brought from Burma by a monk named Huigen in 1,882. The original temple was destroyed during the revolution of overthrowing the Qing Dynasty.

Xin Tian Di (50 minutes ):Situated in the center of Shanghai, south of Huaihai Zhong Road, Shanghai Xin Tian Di is a comprehensive business area of eating, shopping and recreation. It consists of an area of reconstituted traditional Shikumen ("stone gate") houses on narrow alleys, some adjoined book stores houses, shopping malls and cafes and restaurants. Additionally, there are both indoor and outdoor seatings in most of the cafes and restaurants, very westernized.

People Square (40 minutes):Situated in Huangpu District, Shanghai, Shanghai People Square covers an area of about 140,000 square meters. It is made up of an open square, the People's Park and some distinctive buildings. Nowadays, it has become one of the most popular and important landmarks in Shanghai.

Shanghai Museum (2 hours):Located on the People's Square in the Huangpu District of Shanghai, Shanghai Museum is a museum of ancient Chinese art built in 1,952. The original site of the museum was in the Former Shanghai Racecourse club house, moved into the Former Zhonghui Building in 1,600, then, andmoved to People's Square finally .

Old French Concession (1.5 Hours):The Shanghai Old French Concession was a foreign concession in Shanghai from 1849 to 1946, and it was progressively expanded inthe late 19th and early 20th centuries. The area covered by the former French Concession, for much of the 20th century, the premier residential and retail districts of Shanghai, and was also the center of Catholicism in Shanghai.

Day 4 Fri 5-Apr-13

Place & Transport: Shanghai to Zhujiajiao by private car and fly to Xian
Accommodation: Xian City Hotel
Today's Activities: Zhujiajiao Cruise When tour is over, you will be transferred to the airport for your flight to Xian by tour guide and driver. (MU2160,Shanghai-Xian, 6:00pm-8:45pm)
Meals: B,L

Zhujiajiao Cruise (1 Hour):Situated in the western side of Shanghai, Zhujiajiao is 1,700 years old ancient water town. Chinese traditional water towns are notorious for their very old houses lined up along canals and the bridges criss-crossing them. People in Zhujiajiao still  strive to maintain their traditional customes and heritage.

Day 5 Sat 6-Apr-13

Place & Transport: Xian
Accommodation: Xian City Hotel
Today's Activities: Terra Cotta Warriors and Horses Museum,Banpo Museum,Muslim Street
Meals: B,L

Terra Cotta Warriors and Horses Museum (3 hours):The No.1 Attraction in Xian of China, Terra Cotta Warriors and Horses describes the fabulous Qin Dynasty and its first Emperor Qinshihuang to the public with nearly 7,000 life-size pottery soldiers, chariots, weapons and horses.

Muslim Street (1 hour):All restaurants and shops in Muslim Street are managed by Muslims, and the stalls sell different kinds of items. If you are good at bargaining, you will get some products at a quite reasonable price; if you are not, you should also have a try.

Banpo Museum (1 hour):Situated in the eastern suburban district of Xian City of Shaanxi province, Banpo Museum is the first museum constructed at the base of the prehistoric site-Banpo site. Since opened formally in 1958, it has attracted about 2 million people already.

Day 6 Sun 7-Apr-13

Place & Transport: Xian
Accommodation: Xian City Hotel
Today's Activities: Big Wild Goose Pagaoda,Xian City Wall,Xian Bell and Drum Towers Square
Meals: B,L

Xian City Wall (3.5 hours):Xian City Wall is the most complete city wall existig in China, as well as one of the largest ancient military self-defensive systems in the world. It is constructed originally in the early Ming Dynasty (1368-1644).

Big Wild Goose Pagaoda (2 hours):Situated in the southern suburb of Xian, China, Big Wild Goose Pagoda or Dayan Pagoda is a famous ancient construction and a holy place for Buddhists and attracts countless tourists from all over the world for its status in the Buddhist religion.

Xian Bell and Drum Towers Square (30 Minutes):A fenced-in square area between Drum Tower and Bell tower is planted with grass and flowers. In early spring, the tender plum blossoms and bright new grass surrounding the old tower provide a harmonious contrast. Not far from these two Towers, modern shopping malls, boutiques, snack shops around the square reveal the prosperity of the city.

Day 7 Mon 8-Apr-13

Place & Transport: Xian to Beijing by flight
Accommodation: Beijing Longdinghua Hotel
Today's Activities: Hutong Tour in Xicheng District,Summer Palace,Beijing Olympic Green
Meals: B,L

Summer Palace (3 hours):Summer Palace( Yi He Yuan in Chinese) is a common phrase for Garden of Nurtured Harmony. Summer Palace is one of the premier attractions in Beijing and it is also the largest and the best preserved imperial garden in China. In the Summer Palace, you can spend ten minutes to cruise on the Kunming Lake by the ferry boat.

Beijing Olympic Green (2 hours):Located at the north end of the central axis of Beijing City, Beijing Olympic Green contains National Museum, Exhibition area, Olympic Village and Olympic competition avenues. The famous National Stadium, the Bird's Nest and National Aquatics Center are located here.

Hutong Tour in Xicheng District (50 minutes):Hutong is one of the symbols of Beijing in Xicheng District. Hutong is a maze of historic alleyways with one single story traditional courtyard houses. Rickshaw is available in the Hutongs. You can hop one to ride through the narrow alleys to visit the local families to experience the real local life of Beijing.

Day 8 Tue 9-Apr-13

Place & Transport: Beijing Full day tour to Mutianyu Great Wall and Changling
Accommodation: Beijing Longdinghua Hotel
Today's Activities: Mutianyu Great Wall,Changling Tomb
Meals: B,L

Mutianyu Great Wall (4 hours):The Mutianyu Great Wall is about two and a half hours from the center of Beijing City. Mutianyu Great Wall is a place with less visitors but with well-known scenery. Optional cable car is available, and buses to Badaling Great Wall are also available.

Changling Tomb (1 hours):Changling tomb was built in 1413.  It was home to Emperor Yongle and his empress. With an area of 100,000 square meters, Changling is the principal and the best preserved mausoleum among the imperial  mausoleums.

Day 9 Wed 10-Apr-13

Place & Transport: Beijing
Accommodation: Beijing Longdinghua Hotel
Today's Activities: Tiananmen Square,Forbidden City,Temple of Heaven
Meals: B,L

Forbidden City (2-3 Hours):Located in the center of urban area of Beijing City, the Forbidden City is the home to 24 emperors between 1420 and 1911. The great halls, gates and squares contrast with the intricate networks of side courtyards, gardens, halls and quarters are the precious wealth of whole man kind. The nearby attracions are the Wangfujing Street, Tiananmen Square and Shijingshan Park.

Tiananmen Square (50 minutes):Tiananmen Square is the largest square in the world. As it was the site of several key events in the history of China, it has great cultural significance. The attractions closing to Tiananmen Square contain the Forbidden City, museums, governments buildings, mausoleum and Heavenly Gate.

Temple of Heaven (1.5 hours):Temple of Heaven is situated in the southeastern part of Beijing City. It was a place for emperors of Ming and Qing dynasties to host annual ceremonies of prayer to the Heaven for good harvest. Temple of Heaven is a complex of buildings with a flavor of Taoist.

China Tourist Visa Application:

Normally speaking, visitors with regular passport from USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Germany, French etc. to China will only require a tourist visa which will allow them to travel freely in most parts of China as tourists or to visit or accompany Chinese family members. Please make sure that you have got your valid passport and visa before your entry to China. We can send you a free official invitation letter to assist you in obtaining your visa after the reservation deposit is received.

Requirements
1Applicant' passport with at least 6 months remaining validity and available blank pages;
2One truly completed Visa Application Form. If someone else traveling with you shares the same passport, or if you are applying for a visa in a country or territory other than the country of your current nationality, you should fill out the Supplementary Visa Application Form. Please fill in every column of the application form, using "N/A" if not applicable. If the application form is not filled out truly, completely and legibly, this can cause a delay in processing or refusal of the requested visa.
3 A recent passport-size color photo with white background stuck on the application form. For tourism, applicants also need provide the below required documents.
4Under some situations, the original and copy of Chinese Hotel Booking and Return Air Ticket are required.
5For applicants who will travel into Tibet, they must obtain a Tibet Travel Permit. This can either be obtained by joining a group or by obtaining the permit from Tourism Bureau of Tibet Autonomous Region (Tel +86 891 6834313 or Fax +86 891 6834632).

How to Apply
You can come to the Chinese Embassies, Consulates and other Chinese diplomatic missions to submit application in person; if you can't come personally, you can entrust someone else, a travel agency or a visa agency to act on your behalf. Applications by mailing are not acceptable and will be refused at most of Chinese embassies or consulates. No appointment is required.

Processing Time
It regularly takes 4 working days for processing. For express service, additional fees of US$ 20 will be charged per visa for second or third working day releasing. For rush service, additional fees of US$30 per visa will be charged for the same working day releasing.
